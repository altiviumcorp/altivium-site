Vision

Making autonomous flight easier to trust.

Altivium is a student-led startup from Incheon National University focused on the safety layer required for wider industrial drone adoption. We believe unmanned flight can become part of everyday infrastructure only when operators, regulators, and people on the ground can trust how missions are managed.

Focus Areas

Practical safety infrastructure for real operating environments.

01

Operational Safety

Supporting safer drone missions in environments where public trust, repeatability, and fast field decisions matter.

02

Mission Accountability

Helping teams document mission context and strengthen confidence across operators, customers, and public stakeholders.

03

Regulatory Readiness

Building with the expectation that industrial drone operations will require clearer safety practices as the market matures.

Market Direction

Targeting organizations that operate drones near real assets and real people.

Altivium is exploring opportunities with drone training centers, infrastructure inspection teams, logistics operators, and public safety organizations. These groups need more than flight performance; they need systems that help missions become safer, explainable, and easier to approve.

Our early U.S. strategy is centered on partnerships, pilot testing, and customer discovery in regions where public-sector innovation and advanced drone operations are growing together.

01 Industrial Operators Inspection, logistics, construction, and enterprise drone teams
02 Public-Sector Missions Municipal, safety, monitoring, and infrastructure programs
03 Training Ecosystems Professional drone education and field validation partners
