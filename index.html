Company Overview

Safety infrastructure for the age of unmanned flight.

Altivium is a student-led deep tech startup from Incheon National University developing an active safety layer for the industrial drone ecosystem. Our mission is to secure the golden time for pedestrian evacuation while giving operators reliable evidence when accidents happen.

Technology

A self-contained module that detects, alerts, and records.

01

Vision-IMU Cross-Check

Proprietary fusion logic compares inertial and visual data to identify real fall scenarios quickly and reduce false alarms.

02

Independent Active Alert

A 100dB siren and LED strobe run from an independent power source, warning people on the ground even during main battery failure.

03

Blackbox Evidence

High-resolution flight data is preserved internally to support accident investigation, liability defense, and insurance claims.

Market

Built for B2B and B2G drone operators under rising safety expectations.

Altivium bridges the gap between expensive parachute systems and low-end sirens. The product targets commercial drone operators, public safety agencies, municipalities, and FAA Part 107 training centers that need both active pedestrian warning and accountable post-incident data.

Northern Virginia is a strategic entry point for government, public safety, and defense-adjacent drone programs, making NISA a strong soft-landing platform for U.S. expansion.
